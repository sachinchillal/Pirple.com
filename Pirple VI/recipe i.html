Ingredients List :

  • 1 cup of Rice
  • 2 spoon of Oil
  • Chilli Powder
  • Masala Powder
  • Required Vegitable
  • Ginger-Garlic-Mint Paste
  • A pinch of Turmeric Powder
  • Salt
  • 1 cup of Rice
  • 2 spoon of Oil
  • Chilli Powder
  • Masala Powder
  • Required Vegitable
  • Ginger-Garlic-Mint Paste
  • A pinch of Turmeric Powder
  • Salt

Steps :

  1. Take pan, and heat oil for 30s.
  2. Add Required Vegitables to pan, like onions, chilli and tomato; mix and fry those thoroughly.
  3. Then add chilli, turmeric and Masala powder, mix with ginger-garlic paste.
  4. Add salt as per your taste and Stir well
  5. Stir continuously for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Then add a cup of rice to pan, Stir well so that the masala coats the rice.
  7. Your dish is ready to Taste now.
